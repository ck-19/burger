ABOUT BURGER PARK

GREAT FOOD COMES FIRST

Our restaurants are known for serving high-quality,great-tasting and afforadable food.Founded in 1954,BURGER PARK is the second largest fast food hamburger chain in the world with about 18,000 restaurants operating in more than 100 countreies and US territories as at June 30,2019.The original HOME OF THE WHOPPER,our commitment to ingredients,signature recipes, and end-to-end online Burger Park experience for millennials is what has defined our brand for more than 50 years.
